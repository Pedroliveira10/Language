# Methodology

The studio uses short active-recall sessions, immediate feedback, spaced review,
and varied exercise formats. Explanations should be concise and appear after the
learner commits to an answer.

Content should prioritize useful, natural language. Listening material must use
the curriculum locale: `nl-NL`, `pl-PL`, or `pt-PT`. European Portuguese content
must not silently substitute Brazilian vocabulary, pronunciation, or grammar.

Progress is stored locally first. A future account service may synchronize it,
but the app should remain useful offline.
